CruiseMN

Currently there are no resources for people passionate about longboarding to locate the best paved paths to ride in Minnesota. CruiseMN is an app for longboarders to locate paths by map, find exact parking directions, search for paths by keyword, view detailed path information and photos, rate each path, and add additional paths to the current collection.

Try it out: cruise-mn.herokuapp.com

Belwin Citizen Science App

Belwin Outdoor Science is a program for students to experience single-day science-focused field investigations, and currently students must go out into the wilderness with paper copies of data sheets to record nature observations, causing staff to spend time manually collecting and compiling this data. The Belwin Outdoor Science app was created so students are able to reference a photo for organism identification, track their observations, and record any notes electronically while out in nature with no wifi, and teachers are able to consolidate and view current observation data.

Try it out: belwin-citizen-science.herokuapp.com
